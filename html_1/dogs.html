Dogs

  • Dogs are such amazing animals they are such sweet and adorable and they are the best companions.
  • They will always be there with you by your side and love you no matter what happens
  • Their sense of smell is 10,000 to 100,000 times stronger than ours, and they can even dream during sleep, with their unique nose prints acting like fingerprints.
  • Some breeds, like the Basenji, don't bark but make other sounds, while others, like Greyhounds, can run at speeds of up to 45 miles per hour.
  • Dogs have three eyelids and a moist nose that helps them detect scents better.
  • They can also sense human emotions, reacting to our moods.
